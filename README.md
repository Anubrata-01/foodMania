## ğŸ“ Overview

<code>â¯ FoodMania is an innovative and responsive web application that revolutionizes the way people order food online. Inspired by platforms like Swiggy, FoodMania connects users with a wide variety of restaurants, allowing them to browse menus, place orders, and track deliveries in real-time. Whether it's a quick snack or a gourmet meal, FoodMania ensures that delicious food is just a few clicks away.</code>
